About Company

Company LLC "Pallet" is engaged in the manufacturing and sale of wooden pallets. We manufacture wooden pallets according to GOST 33757-2016 and to individual customer sizes; Our production is located in the city of Alexandrov, Vladimir region; We carry out delivery to Moscow, Vladimir, Yaroslavl, Tver, and Ivanovo regions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of pallets do you manufacture?
We manufacture various types of wooden pallets: euro pallets, Finnish pallets, lightweight, reinforced, one-way pallets, and pallets according to individual customer sizes and drawings.
Is it possible to order non-standard size pallets?
Yes, we manufacture pallets according to individual sizes, taking into account load requirements, product type, and operating conditions.
What is the minimum order quantity?
The minimum quantity depends on the pallet type. Usually from 10–50 pieces. Individual terms are possible for large and regular customers.
Are pallets available in stock?
Yes, standard pallets are often available in stock. Please check the current quantity with the manager.
Is it possible to order large volumes on a regular basis?
Yes, we are ready to provide regular deliveries of large volumes according to an agreed schedule.
How long does manufacturing take?
Standard orders are completed within 1–5 business days. The timeframe depends on the volume and complexity of the order.
Do you offer wholesale discounts?
Yes, special prices and discounts apply for wholesale and regular customers.
Is it possible to order urgent manufacturing?
Yes, subject to material availability, we can fulfill an urgent order in the shortest possible time.
What payment methods are available?

Available:

  • Bank transfer
  • Payment by invoice
  • Cash payment (by agreement)
How to place an order?

You can:

  • Submit a request through the form on the website
  • Call by phone
  • Write to email/whatsapp/telegram

Our manager will contact you to clarify the details.
